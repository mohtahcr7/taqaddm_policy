Introduction

At Taqaddum app, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the app.

Use of Data

  • Display personal progress reports and statistics
  • Track financial goals and projects

Local Storage

All data is stored locally on the user's device using Hive technology, and no data is uploaded to external servers. The user has full control over their data.

Security

  • Encryption of local data on the device
  • No sharing of data with any third party
  • Relying on device security to prevent unauthorized access

User Rights

  • Full access to all their data
  • Edit or delete any information at any time
  • Delete the app and all associated data

Children

The app is not intended for children under 13 years old, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update the privacy policy from time to time. Users will be notified of any significant changes through app updates.
